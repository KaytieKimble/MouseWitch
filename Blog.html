The Mouse-Witch

Greetings! My name is Elora Three, and I am, as you might have guessed, a mouse and a witch!

This is me!

I wanted to share a few things you might search for on a misty morning of foraging...

  • The types of things this site carries
  • A sproutling of a tree, wet with morning dew, to be fashioned into a wand once dried and charmed
  • Some red berries for Savhir, the Night of Untold Magic at the eve of the winter season
  • Anything that you know to be not poisonous, but that brings a smell of warmth and loveliness once crushed in a mortar and pestle and added to the mixture for candles
  • Twenty-six or so mushrooms, pale brown in hue
    • These, of course, are for the morn after Savhir
  • Whatever your wee heart desires!
